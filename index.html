Bio

Micah makes A-T Walker, a comic that seeks to elevate awareness about rare genetic diseases that people like his daughter live with. He has also been known to draw a space man or two. He contributed to anthologies such as Not My Small Diary, White Buffalo Gazette, and Comix Accountability Club. He lives in St. Louis Missouri with his wife and 2 children.
