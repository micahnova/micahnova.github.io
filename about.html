Your Name

Painter 路 Mixed Media 路 Contemporary

Your Name is a contemporary artist whose work explores memory, texture, and the quiet drama of everyday spaces. Drawing from architectural forms and natural light, they build layered compositions that invite viewers to slow down and notice the subtle narratives hidden in familiar environments.

Raised in St. Louis and trained in mixed media, Your Name blends collage, pigment, and found materials into tactile surfaces. Recent exhibitions include Held Breath (2024), Substrate (2025), and commissions for community spaces across the Midwest. Their practice centers sustainability, reusing materials to extend their life and meaning.

When not in the studio, Your Name teaches workshops and collaborates with local makers. Upcoming projects include a site-specific installation for the spring season and a limited print series.
